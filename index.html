How it works

Every session follows the same 5-step flow

You describe your problem. The AI figures out everything else.

1
The hook
You describe your problem in plain language. No menus, no modules, no curriculum to browse. Just: what do you want to fix?
2
The diagnostic
The AI asks 5–8 targeted questions to understand your specific numbers and situation. By the time you've answered, you already understand the landscape better — learning begins before the plan.
3
The insight moment Core IP
Before the plan, the AI reveals one personalized, surprising fact calculated from your actual numbers. The moment you realize something about your own situation you couldn't have Googled.
"At your current $95/month minimum on $3,200 at 24% APR, you'll pay $1,240 in interest over 47 months — and your employer is handing you $1,440/year in free money you're currently leaving on the table. That's $2,680/year disappearing from two places you didn't know about."
4
The personalized plan
Step-by-step actions specific to your numbers. Every action has an expandable "why this" explanation — education when you want it, not when you don't.
5
The rabbit hole
Any plan item expands into full depth if you're curious. You pull the education — it never pushes you through a module you didn't ask for.

How it gets smarter

A 3-layer system that learns from every session

Every interaction improves the next one. The product gets smarter the more people use it.

1
Session feedback — immediate
Thumbs up/down + free text after every plan. Low-rated responses get fixed. High-rated flows become templates for everyone.
2
Outcome check-in — 7 days later
"Last week you got a plan to [specific action]. Did you take action?" Captures which plans actually work in the real world vs. which ones sound good but don't get done.
3
Pattern detection — automated
Every off-module question gets logged. When 50+ users ask about the same unrecognized topic, a new module gets drafted automatically — reviewed and approved by the founder before it goes live.
50 users ask same topic → AI drafts module → Founder approves → Ships free to all users
